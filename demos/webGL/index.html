Welcome to this WebGL Demonstration

This site has been created for applicants to our games degree to give you an interactive experience that allows you to experiment with some pretty interesting and advanced technology, but in a highly scaffolded way.

Throughout the demonstration you will have clear instructions on what to do to get the desired effect, whilst also having freedom to experiment with other things to help you understand what is going on.
